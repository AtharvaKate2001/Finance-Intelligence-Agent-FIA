Debt Management

Debt management focuses on structured repayment to minimize financial burden.

Types of Debt:

1. Secured Debt:
Backed by collateral (e.g., mortgages, auto loans).

2. Unsecured Debt:
No collateral required (e.g., credit cards, personal loans).

Repayment Strategies:

1. Debt Snowball Method:
Repay smallest debts first to build momentum.

2. Debt Avalanche Method:
Repay highest-interest debt first to minimize total interest paid.

3. Consolidation:
Combining multiple debts into a single loan with a lower interest rate.

4. Credit Score Impact:
Timely payments improve creditworthiness.

Effective debt management improves long-term financial stability.
