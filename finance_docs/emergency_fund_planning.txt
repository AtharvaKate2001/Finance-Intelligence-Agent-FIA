Emergency Fund Planning

An emergency fund is money set aside to cover unexpected financial situations.

Purpose:
Protect against income disruption, medical expenses, or urgent repairs.

Recommended Amount:
Financial experts recommend saving 3 to 6 months of living expenses.

Where to Store:
Funds should be kept in a liquid and easily accessible account.

Benefits:
- Prevents reliance on high-interest debt.
- Provides psychological security.
- Maintains financial stability during crises.

An emergency fund is a foundational element of financial planning.
