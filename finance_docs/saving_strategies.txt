Saving Strategies

Saving money involves systematically setting aside income for future use.

Key Saving Methods:

1. Pay Yourself First:
Automatically transfer a fixed percentage of income to savings before spending.

2. Automated Savings:
Set recurring transfers to savings accounts to eliminate reliance on willpower.

3. High-Yield Savings Accounts:
These accounts offer higher interest rates compared to traditional savings accounts.

4. Short-Term vs Long-Term Savings:
- Short-Term: Travel, gadgets, emergency repairs.
- Long-Term: Retirement, property purchase, financial independence.

5. Incremental Increases:
Gradually increasing savings rate over time builds strong financial habits.

Savings provide financial flexibility and reduce dependence on credit.
